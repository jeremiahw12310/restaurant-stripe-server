Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

When you use the Dumpling House app, we collect:

  • Account Information: Your name, phone number, and account creation date when you sign up.
  • Loyalty Data: Points earned, rewards redeemed, and receipt scan history.
  • Preferences: Dietary preferences and taste preferences you choose to share for personalized recommendations.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Manage your loyalty rewards and points balance
  • Provide personalized menu recommendations based on your preferences
  • Process reward redemptions
  • Send you relevant updates about your rewards (if enabled)
  • Improve our app and services

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate the app:

  • Firebase (Google): For secure authentication and data storage
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered menu recommendations (no personal data is shared)

These services have their own privacy policies and maintain industry-standard security practices.

Data Security

We take your privacy seriously:

  • All data is transmitted using secure encryption (HTTPS)
  • Your information is stored in secure, access-controlled databases
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties

Your Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Access: View your information in the app at any time
  • Update: Modify your preferences and dietary information
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data through the app's "Delete Account" feature
